Music Video Production

I record music in a studio using Audio-Technica AT4040 Cardiod Condenser Microphone and RME Fireface UFX II interface. Then I mix audio tracks with GarageBand, and use iMovie to edit videos.

I recorded myself singing Beautiful (by Bazzi and Camila Cabello) with a Sennheiser ME66 Shotgun Mic Kit and Zoom H4N Audio Recorder.

I recorded myself singing Green Light (by Lorde) with a Zoom H4N Audio Recorder and Shure SM58-LC Cardioid Dynamic Mic.
